YotaLabs

Lufthansa Systems

Cargo & Load management system used by 3500+ flights and 76 airlines

Business problem

The goal was to reduce fuel costs and benefit from additional payload through trim and payload optimization while simultaneously boosting airline's load controllers’ productivity. The brand new advanced weight and balance solution have to raise the client's load control to the next level by providing full automation, flexible configuration options, and powerful features.

A team of our scientists and engineers was chosen to assist with data science research & development phase. Also, Yotalabs has been working on a web/mobile interface along with the core development team. The goal was to deploy a smart solution to do cargo management in the most efficient way.

”Our solution is one of the world’s leading software solutions for the airline industry. This integrated, modular product line provides optimal support for the entire process of planning and controlling airline resources”

The Challange AKA Business issue

Most travelers, especially frequent flyers, like to cut it close when it comes to getting to the airport. But calculating how long it takes to catch a flight can sometimes feel like an eighth-grade math problem: If Jane needs to be at LAX by 4 p.m., how long will it take her to get there if crawling down the 405 at 40 mph? The difficulty of the equation is compounded when travelers find themselves in unfamiliar cities.
